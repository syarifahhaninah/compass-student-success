# Design Provenance — every decision, its source, and the failure it avoids

Compass borrows deliberately. Each material design decision below cites the documented case it is modelled on and the documented failure it is designed to avoid. (Full benchmarking research with sources sits outside this repo; key references linked inline.)

| # | Compass decision | Modelled on | Avoids |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Reason-coded alerts** — every flag carries human-readable reasons ("no LMS activity 14 days; assessment 2 not submitted"), never an opaque score | Nottingham Trent University's StREAM dashboard practice of engagement transparency | [The Markup (2021)](https://themarkup.org/machine-learning/2021/03/02/major-universities-are-using-race-as-a-high-impact-predictor-of-student-success): EAB Navigate's opaque risk scores used race as a predictor and labelled Black students high-risk at 2–4× the white rate, with advisors untrained on score meaning |
| 2 | **Engagement-first indicators** — behavioural signals drive alerts; demographic and equity attributes are used to *contextualise support and audit fairness*, never as risk inputs | NTU StREAM's deliberate exclusion of demographics; Australian explainable-LA research showing early non-submission is the strongest single leading indicator | Stereotype-driven risk labelling; deficit framing of equity cohorts |
| 3 | **Closed-loop alert lifecycle** — alerts are first-class records with states (raised → triaged → contacted → outcome → re-assessed) and are linked to resulting cases | Georgia State's GPS Advising response protocols (250,000+ alert-triggered advising meetings); QUT's Student Success Program outreach model | The sector's #1 failure: flags nobody actions and no evidence trail — exactly what TEQSA's Support for Students compliance focus audits |
| 4 | **Tiered, capacity-aware triage** — alerts ranked into tiers sized to advisor caseload capacity, not raw threshold dumps | EAB's own post-mortems on alert fatigue | Crude rules ("two absences = at risk") flooding advisors until alerts are ignored |
| 5 | **Matched-comparison intervention evaluation** — contacted vs propensity-matched uncontacted students; never raw before/after | Civitas Learning's prediction-based propensity score matching (PPSM), the industry evaluation standard | [Purdue Course Signals](https://eliterate.us/purdue-course-signals-data-issue-explainer/): the famous "21% graduation boost" collapsed under selection-bias scrutiny |
| 6 | **Ground-truth validation of the evaluator** — the synthetic generator *injects* a known intervention effect, so the matched-comparison method can be shown to recover it | Standard simulation-study practice | Evaluation methods that have never been tested against a known answer |
| 7 | **SEHEEF program-logic tagging at point of entry** — every case record carries a SEHEEF activity type and student life stage | (No documented precedent — this is the novel piece) | The acquittal scramble: reconstructing NBF activity reporting months after the fact |
| 8 | **Weekly leading indicators** (inactivity, non-submission) rather than end-of-semester grades | Open University's OU Analyse weekly predictions; UNE's Automated Wellness Engine (34 triggers, daily, across 8 systems) | Post-mortem analytics that identify at-risk students only after the census date or exam board |
| 9 | **Online-cohort segmentation** throughout | OU Analyse (distance-education scale) | Campus-centric systems blind to online students — urgent for institutions expanding OPM-delivered online cohorts |
| 10 | **Row-level security demo** — advisors see their caseload; managers see all | Standard coordinated-care platform practice (NYU Connect success networks) | "Improving accessibility of student data" without a privacy model |
| 11 | **Fairness audit page** — alert rates, contact rates and outcomes broken down by equity cohort, published in the report itself | IHEP/Gándara guidance on equitable predictive analytics | Silent disparate impact |
| 12 | **First Nations co-design acknowledged as a limitation** — prototype explicitly defers Indigenous alert design to consultation | UNE's Automated Wellness Engine Indigenous alert co-designed with the Oorala Aboriginal Centre | One-size-fits-all alerting imposed on First Nations students |
| 13 | **Official metric definitions** — DoE adjusted attrition, retention, success rate (EFTSL passed / EFTSL certified) with TCSI-style element references | Department of Education Higher Education Statistics methods; ACSES benchmarking practice | League-table comparisons that ignore demographic mix; every team inventing its own "retention rate" |
| 14 | **In-house, stack-native build** (Python + SQL + Power BI on a Microsoft-shaped environment) | The "IKEA effect" observed when institutions replace vendor scores with homegrown systems (GSU, UNE, OU, Deakin LA4T); ACU's existing Azure + Power BI DataHub | Vendor black boxes with poor adoption and no institutional ownership |
